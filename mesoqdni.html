Carnivores

Meat eating dinosaurs were called CARNIVORES. There were about 100 types of dinosaurs who ate meat. Spinosaurus (means Spine Lizard) was the largest meat eating dinosaur, even bigger than the T-Rex. There are little and big type carnivores. They can be with different things on their body. For example there are some with feathers on and so on.
